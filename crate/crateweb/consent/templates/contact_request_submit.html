Submit patient contact request

You can submit contact requests for studies for which all of the following are true:

  • You are the lead or other designated researcher (or you are the RDBM)
  • Title/forename/surname information is available for the lead researcher
  • The study has been approved patient contact
  • A patient information leaflet has been uploaded by the RDBM

The RDBM can submit contact requests using NHS numbers or research IDs; researchers are restricted to research ID lookup.
